Stake to a signer

Locks your own STX toward a signer by calling pox-5 stake (any wallet — not admin-gated). No pool manager and no aggregate-commit — naming the signer-manager attributes your stake to that signer, and the lock takes effect next reward cycle. On this network the STX lock is subject to post-conditions, so this sends a deny-mode STX post-condition equal to the staked amount.
