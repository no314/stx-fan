Register-self

Broadcasts register-self(signer-manager, signer-key, auth-id, signer-sig) from your admin wallet. Runs the same live checks Sidekick does before signing: pinned PoX-5 contract, message-hash recovery, signature validity, and grant-not-yet-used. Get the signer output by running stacks-signer generate-staking-signature --signer-manager <manager> --auth-id <id> --json on the signer host.

Generate on the signer host (private key stays on the node):
stacks-signer generate-staking-signature --config /etc/stacks-signer/config.toml --signer-manager <manager> --auth-id <id> --json
Adjust the config path to yours. Confirm flags with --help for your build. For testing, dApp 2 (offline key helper) can also produce this JSON.
