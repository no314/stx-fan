Claim rewards

Yield is sBTC and is claimed in two steps. Both calls are permissionless — anyone can run either for anyone (you pay only the tx fee; the sBTC always goes to the staker, never the caller).

Stakeryou

Step 1 (claim-rewards) pulls the signer's whole cycle reward from PoX-5 into the signer-manager contract. Step 2 (claim-staker-rewards) pays one staker their share out of the signer-manager contract. Step 1 must happen once per cycle before step 2 — if nobody has run it, there is nothing to claim yet. Since both are permissionless, a staker can run step 1 themselves and then step 2.

This tool manages STX staking rewards. The sBTC bond parameters (bond periods, bond index) are for the Bitcoin-staking side and are left empty here — they apply only to sBTC bond positions, which this tool doesn't cover.

Runs in originator post-condition mode: nothing can leave your (the caller's) wallet without a post-condition, while the manager and PoX-5 contracts are still allowed to move the sBTC reward to the staker. You send nothing, so none of your own assets can move.

Pool operator — check who was staking (advanced, slow)

A full roster of a pool's stakers cannot be built in-browser from the public API (the contract-events endpoint times out). Enter the addresses you want to check — your own, or a candidate list from your own node/indexer — and this verifies each against the manager + cycle using fast read-only calls. Loading a long list can take a while.

Not claiming does not forfeit rewards — a staker's share is held in the manager and can't be swept by the admin. sBTC without a pox-addr; native L1 BTC if the staker set one. Testnet = Tier 1, mainnet = Tier 2.
